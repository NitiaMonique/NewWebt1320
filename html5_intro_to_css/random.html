Wakanda Forever!

Sadly, Black Panther Wakanda Forever had to go without Chadwick Boseman. There was so much talk about who would takeover the black panther role. Ultimately, his sister, Shuri, played by actress Letitia Wright, now dons the black panther name. She battles herself as she tries to adjust to the loss of her brother while also battling an enemy just as powerful and resourceful as the world she comes from. A war of worlds begin.
